Hello,

I found a solution to my problem, Dane advised me to use metatiles but 
this is not efficient.

A solution is add a gutter parameter in layer option.

As my images have a size of 30pxl,  the gutter should be at least 30pxl.

This solution generates images larger but the tile is correct.

http://openlayers.org/dev/examples/gutter.html

Alex
