Do you want a shapefile of the outline/border of Venezuela or  
administrative units within Venezuela?

For the former try downloading the thematic mapping world borders  
shapefile [1] and you can extract the country of Venezuela.

For the latter try World Resources data [2].

For further questions about open/public gis data I'd suggest posting  
to the OSGEO GEODATA list [3].

Cheers,

Dane

[1] http://thematicmapping.org/downloads/
[2] http://www.globalforestwatch.org/english/datawarehouse/index.asp#Venezuela
[3] http://lists.osgeo.org/mailman/listinfo/geodata
