Hi Rainer,

Here something to try first. Are you building Mapnik from source? If so, 
you might get significant performance improvements by passing an extra 
parameter to postgis :
    p['estimate_extent'] = 'true' but it's only ~95% accurate.

I'm not familiar with cherrypy, is it behind mod_python? Depending on 
your configuration it is possible that WMS pools too many DB connections.

These are just some preliminary thoughts. Could you provide some more 
details about your setup and data?
