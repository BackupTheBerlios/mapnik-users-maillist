Hi,

on maps, objects often overlap. In most cases, this is not relevant
(e.g. names in front of places, ways through wood etc). Sometimes
however, one gets a wrong impression. I have put such an example at
http://alius.ayous.org/~aba/image.png

Would it be possible to apply a (dynamic) transformation to the map so
that problematic ways get moved apart enough that both ways of the
conflicting pair are fully present?

For me, a large street (or a railway) will always be an important land
marker, so it's better if the map is a bit non-linear than if they'd
disappear (like they do now). (And in case one is interessted in correct
distances, as soon as one zoom in, the map will get more correct
anyways.)
