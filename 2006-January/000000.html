Happy New Year and Welcome to Mapnik's users list!

Mapnik is coming along. I've released 0.2.4a (more about it on the site 
http://mapnik.berlios.de).
This year I've got lots of plans for new features/improvements and I'm 
looking forward to stepping up the pace. I hope this list will become 
central to discussion of all things "mapnik" in particular and FOSS GIS 
in general.

I also started working on a GUI application using Qt4. There are some 
screen-shots on the site. I haven't published the source yet, but if 
anyone is interested to collaborate or just try it out I can try to 
speed things up.
Please let me know.

Also, I reckon mapnik is nearly ready for win32 and mac. Any win32/mac 
fans out there?

Anyway, feel free to throw at me any questions regarding mapnik and I 
will try to answer them the best I can.

All the best
Artem

P.S. Gem.Tang, if you're receiving this, thanks again for digging into 
the source and bringing important issues to the surface. I made changes 
to the point_impl to not use vertex_vector and I use boost::thread lib 
for all synchronization needs. Other changes: ref_ptr is replaced  by 
shared_ptr and there is serialization support for map objects.
