>First, like Artem said, make sure to *install* boost, rather than trying to configure against its libs in the 'stage' directory.
>
>Also, when you write back make sure to tell us where you installed it.
>
>So, if you have some other boost in /usr/local/ perhaps you should use some custom prefix like: /opt/boost-43
>

>python scons/scons.py BOOST_INCLUDES=/opt/boost-43/include BOOST_LIBS=/opt/boost-43/lib
>
>DO NOT use the BOOST_VERSION option (it does not do what you think).

>If the above does not help you work things out then please post your "config.log" somewhere we can look at it.
