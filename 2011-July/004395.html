Hi Pieter,

Not a bug but rather a feature. You can modify viewer.ini file to have
something like :

[mapnik]
plugins_dir=/usr/local/lib/mapnik/input/
fonts/1/dir=/usr/local/lib/mapnik/input/
fonts/size=1

HTH,
Artem
