Hey Mapnik Users,

I recently finished up the first version of a Mapnik-generated map of the
Sierra Nevada Range in California, USA.  During the creation of this map
I've found this mailing list (and the entirety of the Mapnik community)
very helpful and a good source of ideas so I figured I'd share my map.  The
URL is:

http://www.closedcontour.com

The motivation behind the map can be found in my blog (linked from the
above site) but put simply, I want to create a modern version of the USGS
topos tailored to outdoor recreation.  This first version includes summits
of the SPS Peak List and trails throughout the range.  

I hope to add data and refine the map for the foreseeable future and am
open to comments and suggestions.

Thanks,
Dan
