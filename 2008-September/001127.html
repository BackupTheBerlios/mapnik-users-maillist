Mapnik-users,

The last month has seen a huge spike in Mapnik users contributing to  
the Trac Wiki:

http://trac.mapnik.org/timeline

With some notable page additions for new users such as:

http://trac.mapnik.org/wiki/IntroductionToGIS
http://trac.mapnik.org/wiki/ExampleCode
http://trac.mapnik.org/wiki/MapDesign
http://trac.mapnik.org/wiki/MapnikRenderers

But, we can do even better. So, this is a call out to all current  
users to contribute their mapnik knowledge, tips, tricks, and ideas to  
further the value of Mapnik trac.

We are looking for help to round out the documentation particularly on  
these pages:

http://trac.mapnik.org/wiki/LinuxInstallation
http://trac.mapnik.org/wiki/LabelingSupport (all the links from this  
page)
http://trac.mapnik.org/wiki/SymbologySupport
http://trac.mapnik.org/wiki/OutputFormats

So, go to http://trac.mapnik.org/register to get your trac account set  
up if you do not already have one, and then add your knowledge by  
editing existing pages or adding new ones.

Thanks,

Dane
