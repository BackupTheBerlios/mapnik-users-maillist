I looked at both the images. After your DB Update, the roads which were
'blue' and 'dull yellow' previously are visible in the new image. Rest all
of them disappeared. This can happen in two conditions: 
1. Either you didn't populated all the roads with all the road classes you
were using earlier.
2. Or, there is some problem with your query/filter statement in xml
mapfile. 

Please look into the data again, and I am sure you will find the blunder :)
