Hi,

The problem is before QGIS 1.1 python plugins could not get access to the postgres password (http://trac.osgeo.org/qgis/changeset/10581).

So, the only way Quantumnik can work with QGIS less than 1.1 is if your postgres tables do not have a password.

Or, you can upgrade QGIS.

Dane
