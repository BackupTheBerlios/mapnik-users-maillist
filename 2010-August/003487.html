Okay. Points placed, in this case always represent single entities.  
Your assumption that they are grouped/multiple is reasonable if  
clustering were happening (combining of two features a given distance  
apart into one rendered point). But it is not as right now points that  
are placed simply exclude other points that might later overlap when  
rendered and thus the sorting of your data determines which will more  
likely show up.

I agree this would be a desirable feature (clustering) and I actually  
started scoping it for a client last week. I will reply to this thread  
when I have made progress.
