On Aug 25, 2010, at 7:02 AM, Mak Kolybabi wrote:

> Is there a way to get the actual bytes that represent a PNG image without saving a view to disk?

Both a mapnik.Image [1] and mapnik.ImageView [2] have an im.tostring('[format]') method. If passed no argument they spit back raw bytes, otherwise they encode in the format supplied.

>>> from mapnik import Image
>>> im = Image(256,256)
>>> im.tostring('png256')
