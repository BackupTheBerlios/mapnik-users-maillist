The GeoDjango installer uses GEOS from the PostGIS binaries, so that's
probably a good place to look.

http://postgis.refractions.net/download/windows/#windbinaries

I think all you need is to drop the GEOS dll (libgeos_c-1.dll) into
somewhere on your system path.
