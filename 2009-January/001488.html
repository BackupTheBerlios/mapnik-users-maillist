Hi Damian,
Looks like windows doesn't have HOME environment variable set. You can set
it yourself in console, something like:
set HOME=C:\Users\

Or just remove it from generate_tiles.py and modify mapfile and tile_dir to
have full paths.

Also, consider subscribing to mapnik-users.
