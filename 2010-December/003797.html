"Learning" tag has never been actively used in the osm.xml, and therefore it looks like it was removed from the default.style for osm2pgsql [1]

So, you could either remove "learning" from your Mapnik SQL subselect in your stylesheet or you add back the "learning" column to your default.style, then re-import the data.

Dane

[1] http://trac.openstreetmap.org/changeset/23678/applications/utils/export/osm2pgsql/default.style
