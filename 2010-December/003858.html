After a nice time looking at mapnik's code,  I managed to totally disable collision detection for line_placed labels.Since I don't know nothing about C++, I made it in a "ugly" way changing the label_collision_detector.hpp making has_placement aways return true. Maybe it could become an option in the future.More details in tiket #678.
Look at the before and after images. It's a another steep heading toward nice 'on-the-fly' typographic maps made from OSM data. lol
