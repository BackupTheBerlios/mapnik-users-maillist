Hi,

You can also find the Well Known Text presentation of the polygon and
use it as a geometry in PostGIS spatial query.  Well known text is most
easy to get by opening the shapefile with OpenJUMP, selecting the
polygon and doing right click - Feature info.

I can't test the query right now, but see examples for example from
http://www.cse.iitb.ac.in/dbms/cep/day8/lab/postgis.php

You may need to give also SRID with the WKT polygon.  And you can run
the whole query with OpenJUMP and get the result directly as a map.

> 
>   SELECT names, Centroid(the_geom) FROM roads
