> bbox(1795035.9102178386,8402459.259925343,3696374.0218113307,9864708.395856721)

> It is possible that Mapnik works OK, but there may be nothing to render inside the less than 100 x 100 metre boxes you gave.  You can check coordinates around the area that interests you with a map at:

>
