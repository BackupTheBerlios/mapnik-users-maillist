Richard Ive wrote:
> For anyone who is using OSM to render the world:
> 
> Out of curiosity; what spec machines have people got to render the 
> entire world, and how long did it take to do it from zoom 0 to 18?

Haven't done it myself, but I just know that what you describe would be 
... laborious and painful.

Importing the whole planet can take anywhere from some 12 hours or so to 
over a week, depending on your machine specs. Plenty of memory is key. 
You'd need 12+ GB to make the import go smoothly.

Then the case of rendering all tiles. Why? Did you happen to find an 
actual and real use case for this? Most of us do on-demand rendering, 
with the option of prerendering only the lower zoom levels after the import.

Rendering every tile for the world, z0-z18 takes many days(/weeks) and 
would require a serious amount of storage, IIRC well over 1 TB.
