Because I do not know anything about asc files nor paletted colours this may go all wrong, but I'll have a try.

-expand option is for 1 band images having a colour table. Check if your image has such (or if gdal recognises it) with gdalinfo. 

One way to make a 3-band image out of 1-band image is to pick the only band three times with
gdal_translate -b 1 -b 1 -b 1 1-band.tif 3-band.tif.  Result will have three channels, but it will look like a greyscale.

By the way, creation option for tiled tiffs is TILED=YES.
