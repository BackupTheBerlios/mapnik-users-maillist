This is a tough problem I am struggling with for a couple of days : how to
make my roads junction neat while taking care of their elevation ?

 

The main problem is related to the data I am using (TeleAtlas shape files).

drawn first/last in order to have bridges above tunnels and not the
opposite.

Well I have one but it would need to make a script with quite sophisticated
queries and data sorting that I would like to avoid.

main large roads.

But if I choose a different order to draw roads elements I will have those
dripping problems again.

 

I see on OpenStreetMap, as well as on Google Maps that they can draw
