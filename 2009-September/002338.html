I replied on your previous message. I build mapnik with my own version of
GDAL. I add others dll like ogdi or geos. 
So, to build mapnik with ogr/gdal, people have to add in the execution
folder all the ddl files used by gdal/ogr.

> walker tool (http://www.dependencywalker.com) against the ogr.input (I did
> it with gdal.input, renaming it in gdal.dll) and found the DLL I was
> missing.

> plugin directory most of the time)
