> bad lexical cast: source type value could not be interpreted as target
>

looks good. You just need to put single-quotes around numbers and add  
'format' parameter.(All parameters are expected to be strings at this  
moment) :

layer.datasource = mapnik.Raster( file='yell_15sep99_bands432.tif',  
type='tiff', lox='487755.812', loy='4886394.5', hix='593235.812',  
hiy='4994874.5')
