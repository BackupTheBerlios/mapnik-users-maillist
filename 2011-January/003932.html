Hi all,

I was a participant in the first Mapnik code sprint, had a lot of fun in it, 
and even managed to get some work done!

The idea was to hold a second code sprint somewhere in early 2011. The issue 
hasn't moved forward, so I'd like to propose something.

I've been talking with the chaps at Medialab Prado[1], a medialab/hackspace in 
Madrid. And they'd be happy to let us use their purpose-built hackspace[2], 
and with a bit of luck, let us display a map in their 8-meter-tall screen[3].

[1] http://medialab-prado.es/
[2] http://www.flickr.com/photos/medialab-prado/4031060209/
[3] http://www.flickr.com/photos/medialab-prado/4055741426/

The only issue is that the place is closed on Sundays, and they hold a thing 
called OpenLab (which is noisy and with lots of people) on Fridays.

So, my proposal is to hold a code sprint on a Monday-Tuesday-Wednesday in the 
upcoming months. I know it's sub-optimal to not hold the code sprint in a 
weekend, so *please* do propose other venues.

If you don't propose another venue, I'd love to know if this Madrid proposal 
makes any sense to you :-)
