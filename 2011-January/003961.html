Hi,

Try to localise the problem. Start for example by converting a small window (same you used with Mapnik) from the .vrt file into geotiff with gdal_translate. If the result is good then the problems is probably in how Mapnik treats .vrt. If GDAL makes odd result as well then the reason is probably in the .vrt file of something related to the originals. I can only say that I have used .vrt files for combining thousands of original images and results have been good both with GDAL tools and through Mapserver WMS and WCS services.
I could also be good to start from the most simple case. Take two adjacent images and check them visually and with gdalinfo to see they are OK. Then make a .vrt from those two and see what happens.
