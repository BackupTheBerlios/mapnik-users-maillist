Anisha,

I think you need to move a bit slower :) Take some time to understand  
that data is in different map projections [1] and you can't just  
switch out data exported from postgres (that I assume is in EPSG: 
900913) with a shapefile that works with a tutorial that is in EPSG: 
4326, without also modifying the XML 'srs' parameter.

See http://trac.mapnik.org/wiki/IntroductionToGIS for more details.

I might recommend to you to check out QuantumGIS. http://qgis.org.  
QGIS provides an interface to allow you to load up different data, and  
does a descent job of automatically interpreting the projection  
(Although note that is does not understand EPSG:900913).
