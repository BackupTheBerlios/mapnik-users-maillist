Hello,

As far as I know, there isn't such feature.
You have to use a different font like "DejaVu Sans Bold" or "DejaVu Sans
Oblique" to enable bold or italic.

Here is the list of available fonts inside the "/font" mapnik directory.
"DejaVu Sans Book", 
"DejaVu Sans Bold", 
"DejaVu Sans Oblique",
"DejaVu Sans Bold Oblique",
"DejaVu Sans Condensed",
"DejaVu Sans Condensed Bold", 
"DejaVu Sans Condensed Oblique", 
"DejaVu Sans Condensed Bold Oblique",
"DejaVu Sans Extralight",
"DejaVu Sans Mono Book",
"DejaVu Sans Mono Bold",
"DejaVu Sans Mono Oblique",
"DejaVu Sans Mono Bold Oblique",
"DejaVu Serif Book",
"DejaVu Serif Bold",
"DejaVu Serif Italic",
"DejaVu Serif Bold Italic",
"DejaVu Serif Condensed",
"DejaVu Serif Condensed Bold",
"DejaVu Serif Condensed Bold Italic"

You can also add you own .ttf font files and use them.

Nicolas.
