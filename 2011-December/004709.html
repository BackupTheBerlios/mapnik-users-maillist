It sounds like perhaps you're successfully generating the tiles at very low
zooms because they probably get their data from shapefiles (that's certainly
true for OSM), but that tiles which are zoomed in further will also use
queries against PostGIS and those queries are failing.

Try double-checking the queries in the mapnik stylesheet to ensure that
they're valid. Also check that you've got permission to access Postgres and
check the Postgres logs for errors that may be happening.
