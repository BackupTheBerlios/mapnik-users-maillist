Hi,

Yes, I have a goal of adding Mapnik read support for a variety of  
other style and layer descriptors including:

1) Quantum GIS project files
2) SLD / and perhaps Geoserver data (featuretypes/catalog.xml)
3) MapServer Mapfiles

My work so far has been primarily at the QGIS level as I've been  
working on integrating Mapnik into QGIS as an alternate rendering  
engine.

I'd be happy to give you an estimate of the contract time it would  
take to implement each part of SLD support.

Things that would need to be done in addition to the parsing of basic  
SLD FeatureTypeStyles would be designing logic for associating styles  
with Mapnik layers, handling the different Filter Syntax used between  
Mapnik and SLD, and the mapping of potential SLD VendorOptions to the  
right Mapnik equivalent symbolizer properties (such as 'followLine' or  
'maxDisplacement').

Contact me off list if you would like to discuss further.

Dane
