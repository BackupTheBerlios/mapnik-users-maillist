No. There are a variety of tickets on trac requesting variants on the  
idea of line offsets, but not relating to coloring sides of lines  
differently.

If PostGIS/GEOS even supports single sided buffers
(http://trac.osgeo.org/geos/ticket/215 
) you might be able to do this dynamically by drawing a line twice,  
once as a single-side buffered polygon and again as a normal line on  
top, essentially 'casing' the line only on one side.
