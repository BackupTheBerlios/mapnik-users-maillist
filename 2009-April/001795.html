>
> Presuming you're on 0.6.0, change
>
> <Map srs="+proj=lcc +datum=NAD83" bgcolor="steelblue">
>
> to
>
> <Map srs="+proj=lcc +datum=NAD83" bgcolor="steelblue" buffer_size="128">
>
> Or some other value instead of 128 that's large enough to make your labels
> uncut. This will make mapnik fetch and render a larger bbox for your tiles,
> and then discard this gutter area to create your tile.
