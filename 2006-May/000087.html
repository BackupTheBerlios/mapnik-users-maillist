> Thanks for compliments:P.  At the moment there is no control on how text

> adjusted lines  representing a street/river you will  end up with
> repeated  names (unless there are overlapping ). We can improve this
> behavior by introducing common attribute and render only  _one_  text

> I'll be very interested to hear other ideas regarding
> text labels positioning and now we can improve Mapnik.

What I would love to see is an option to specify the minimal distance
(in pixels, radius) between two labels. Probably more or less similar
to the collision detection but with a variable radius. That way, for
example, a river would always be identified by 4 labels on a 800x600px
map, regardless of the extent.
