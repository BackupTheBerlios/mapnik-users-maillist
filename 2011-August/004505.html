What output do you get from the following two commands on your system:

$ mapnik-config --cflags
$ mapnik-config --libs

I guess Mapnik should be including the location of the boost headers in
those cflags. The mod_tile Makefile could perhaps be changed to pick
them up from there automatically like it does for freetype:

RENDER_CPPFLAGS += $(shell freetype-config --cflags)
