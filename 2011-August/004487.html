I had this problem when I first tried to render contours, and the problem
was the projection - the contours were in lat/lon and the rest of the data
mercator.  This meant that the contours layer needed a projection string
like:
+proj=latlong +datum=WGS84
compared to the OSM data layers that have a projection like:
+proj=merc +a=6378137 +b=6378137 +lat_ts=0.0 +lon_0=0.0 +x_0=0.0 +y_0=0
+k=1.0 +units=m +nadgrids=@null +no_defs

You might not have made the same mistake though!
