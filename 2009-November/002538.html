Niklas Cholmkvist wrote:
> Is it possible for mapnik to render the key:name with the key:name:en
> in parenthesis, for all points and ways? Like this example:
> key:name (key:name:en)

This is really an OSM question, not a mapnik question as such.

> or like this example:
> Θεσσαλονίκη (Thessaloniki)

Technically, this is possible, but it's currently not done this way on 
the main OSM 'mapnik' map. On the one hand, why would it have to be 
name:en in parentheses, and not any of the other major world languages? 
And on the other hand, it would make the labels larger and clutter the 
map even more.

Wikimedia is currently working on maps rendered in specific languages.

http://meta.wikimedia.org/wiki/OpenStreetMap

http://cassini.toolserver.org/tile-browse/browse-en.html?zoom=11&lat=37.97803&lon=23.78128&layers=B
