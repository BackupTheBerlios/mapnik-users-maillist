Peter,

I owe you a huge thanks and an apology. The thanks is for being persistent! I apologize because I was absent minded and thought a fixed a problem that I never did and is clearly the cause of your heartache.

Turns out that I when I made a change to optimize the speed of writing from a Mapnik image into the QGIS canvas this caused all maps to disappear on windows. I noticed this soon after making the change and thought I fixed it and re-released, but I clearly didn't and just dreamed that up. So, I've finally done that now. I've re-released Quantumnik 0.3.9 which should allow the maps to show up for you on windows.

You should be able to update easily within the QGIS plugin manager.

There surely were and likely still are issues that can cause layers to disappear when projections are assigned by QGIS improperly, but at least I can confirm that the sample map you sent me works just fine on my machine (Mac OS X) so I'm confident this will fix the problem for you.

Dane
