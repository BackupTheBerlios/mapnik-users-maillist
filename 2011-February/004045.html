Greetings,

I have a project around custom maps and am looking for developers and/or
designers who might be interested in helping out as a paid contract project.


I am looking to build a web-based tool where someone can generate a custom
map for a small area (1/2 mile to maybe 50 mile total visible area) with
limited control over what it looks like, colors used, data layers to show
(police, subways, parks, rivers, etc). The system will be an easy to use
tool whose final output would just be a static image or perhaps a limited
set of tiles to support 1-3 zoom levels of the same area. The generated maps
would primarily be in the United State initially but looking to cover
Canada, West. Europe, Australia after that.

If this is of interest to you, and if it would be something you are
available in the near term to work on, please give me a reply by email and I
can explain in greater detail what I am looking for.
