> can add some streets and street names. Would you give an advice how to do

You can edit the online datastore with josm or merkaartor [1], and
after you must update your data on PostgreSQL, also you can edit the
data on your local database with qgis [2], if you choose this solution
remember that you don't edit the online data and you don't help the
community of OSM
