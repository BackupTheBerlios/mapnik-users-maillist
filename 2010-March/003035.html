Hi Dom,

Try adding :  -lboost_system to the list of libraries e.g


g++ -o utils/shapeindex/shapeindex utils/shapeindex/shapeindex.o
 src/envelope.o plugins/input/shape/shapefile.o -Lagg -Lsrc -L/usr/lib64
 -L/usr/local/lib64 -L/opt/icu/lib -L/usr/local/lib -lboost_program_options
 -lboost_iostreams -lboost_filesystem -lboost_system
