Francesco,

You could certainly merge those rules, but they appear to display the icon at a different size, so depending on the goal of the style perhaps you don't want to merge them.

Also, only rules that match at a given scale are evaluated, so if your rule scales are exclusive, merging them would not speed up rendering.

Dane
