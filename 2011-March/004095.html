You are doing nothing wrong, and your setup seems to be complete. As in:
you can generate a map.

However, your local database is not as recent as OSM itself is. When did
you import your db from the geofabrik extract? When you update your local
db with a recent OSM extract, you'll get the same map output as osm.org.

PS: As a data issue, one of the OSM lists would be a better place to
discuss this.
