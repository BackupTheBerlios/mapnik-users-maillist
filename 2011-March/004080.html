Yes, sorry about this. Looks like you've found a bug.

In general is it common for symbols to not appear with the MarkersSymbolizer because they may overlap. So, you can prompt them to show up if you use:

allow-overlap="true"

But, in testing just now I notice that with large symbols that still does not ensure placement (despite overlap) so something is wrong in the markers placement code.

So, could you do two things?

1) try setting allow-overlap="true" and making sure your svg symbols are small, like 5-10 px wide. Then let me know whether that allows them to show up.

2) Next file a ticket against Mapnik 2.0 milestone at trac.mapnik.org with any and all details you can provide (like your symbol files and the source of the data).
