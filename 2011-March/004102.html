Mapnik is a renderer, openlayers is used to visualize the results in a
web browser. To see how to utilize rasters in mapnik, have a look
here:
http://trac.mapnik.org/wiki/RasterSymbolizer

As you note correctly, you might have to reproject the images before
you can use them in mapnik.
