Hi,

This does not have to do with this certain problem, but according to the WMS standard layer names must not begin with underscore nor number even they are allowed later in the name. Therefore theoretically &LAYERS=__all__ is invalid. However, I do not know any schmema validating WMS client that would refuse to use __all__ as a layer name.
