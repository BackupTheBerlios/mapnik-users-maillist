I have successfully compiled mapnik2 on CentOS 6.0.

Now I am trying to generate map using sample files:

1) world_map.py:
#!/usr/bin/env python

from mapnik2 import *

mapfile = 'world_styles.xml'
map_output = 'image2.png'
projection = '+proj=latlong +datum=WGS84'

m = Map(600, 300)
load_map(m, mapfile)

            <LineSymbolizer>
