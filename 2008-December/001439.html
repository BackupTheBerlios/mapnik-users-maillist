Hi,

Looks like you're missing the "AS" part to give the field a name. In  
the comment I called it "name_stretched", which is what your  
TextSymbolizer name paramter needs to be:
	... REGEXP_REPLACE(UPPER(name), '(.)', E'\\1\xC2\xA0\xC2\xA0', 'g')  
AS name_stretched ...

and:
	<TextSymbolizer name="name_stretched" ...

-mike.
