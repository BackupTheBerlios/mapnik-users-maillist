Well Kosmos does it like this "Width=1:1;9:1.5;17:6". Which means 1
pixel for zoomlevel 1, 1.5 pixels on zoomlevel  9 and 6 pixels on
zoomlevel 17. You can give as much different widths as you want. As
mapnik uses scaledenominator, it coulde be something like this, For
example motorways <denominator>25000000:1, 5000000:1.5, 1000000:2,
500000:2.5</denominator>
