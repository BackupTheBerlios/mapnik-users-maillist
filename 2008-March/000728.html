Ok, that suggestion is correct! I compiled with

python scons/scons.py  PGSQL_INCLUDES=/usr/include/postgresql \
PGSQL_LIBS=/usr/lib

and after installing the mapnik library the generate_image.py from the osm 
mapnik tree accesses the database and a lot of stuff is drawn.
