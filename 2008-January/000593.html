Hello Attila,

On 30 Jan 2008, at 01:09, Attila Csipa wrote:

> I'm looking into Mapnik as an alternative to render some labeled  
> polylines,
> however ran into some basic issues which I guess/hope is more of a  
> result me
> not being to find the right examples/docs. So, to the point, could  
> somebody
> give me a hint as to how does one go about putting putting multiple  
> static
> labels on a polyline feature ? For example, a polyline (with no  
> particular
> feature data associated) that has 'water' written on it every now  
> and then.


You can use 'spacing' parameter (TextSymbolizer) to render multiple  
labels along polyline.

For example (in Python) :

sym = TextSymbolizer('name', 'DejaVu Sans Book', 10, Color('black'))
sym.label_placement =  label_placement.LINE_PLACEMENT
sym.spacing  = 100 #  distance between repeated labels on a single  
geometry
