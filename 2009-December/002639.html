http://spatialreference.org/ref/epsg/3785/

However, that code is not widely supported yet.  I believe EPSG:900913 is still the most common in use.  Best to keep the spatialrefererence page open and learn to give thy cryptic WKT or Proj4 strings if nothing else helps.
