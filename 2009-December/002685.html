Hello,

For your shield/railway station problem draw the railway station first.

For you crossroads, make two stiles. One for the casing of all your road,
the other one for filling.

The order you add styles to the map defines the order in which they will be
drawn? First added, first drawn.

Have a look here for more info : http://trac.mapnik.org/wiki/MapDesign

Nicolas.
