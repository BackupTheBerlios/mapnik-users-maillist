Exactly, 

For low sized shapefiles, always go for Shapefiles instead of PostGIS. It is
because, at the time of map generation, complete shapefiles gets loaded into
the memory (if memory is good enough). But, with PostGIS, its DB look up for
each time. 

Go for the shapefiles !!
