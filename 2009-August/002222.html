On Aug 21, 2009, at 8:51 AM, Jome Akpoduado wrote:

> How do I post a complete step by step guide on the trac wiki for  
> those who may be going through what I went through. :D
> Thanks
> Jome
>


1) Register on the trac wiki:

https://trac.mapnik.org/register

2) Login

3) go to a page url of your choosing that does not exist (using  
CamelCase page name):

e.g. https://trac.mapnik.org/wiki/WmsFaq

4) start editing another page that looks like it might offer a good  
example of what you want to write:

https://trac.mapnik.org/wiki/InstallationTroubleshooting

5) Copy and paste some text out of that page as starter material for  
formatting your new page

6) write like a wild man

7) the page name will automatically become a link if placed somewhere  
on another page.

The folks in #mapnik irc will see your edits stream into the chat room  
so others will help correct anything you might be uncertain about.

- dane
