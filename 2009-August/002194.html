Mapnik-users,

I'm going to be mostly offline for the remainder of August through  
Oct, so I've pushed a few releases out for Nik2img and Quantumnik:

For nik2img [1] just do:

$ easy_install -U nik2img

And Quantumnik 0.1.6 should now be available as an upgrade in the QGIS  
plugin manager.

Notably, Quantumnik now supports exporting to Cairo formats, live  
preview of the Mapnik XML, improved support for a variety of OGR  
formats, and a nicer interface for exporting Map images (still lots of  
print stuff left to do). See the screenshot: http://dbsgeo.com/images/rendering_flickr_alpha_shapes_via_mapnik.png

Enjoy,

Dane

[2] quantumnik changelog: http://bitbucket.org/springmeyer/quantumnik/src/tip/CHANGELOG.txt
