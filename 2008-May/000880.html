On May 1, 2008, at 1:48 PM, Paul Smith wrote:

> Mike:
>
> I'm not following the gentlemen at firetree.net: there's no copy the  
> boost libs to a new directory. There is some naming impedance  
> mismatch which you can correct with the script below.
>
>     #!/bin/sh
>     for lib in `find /usr/lib -name 'libboost_*-gcc-mt-1_33_1.so'`; do
>         name=`echo ${lib} | sed -r -e 's/.*boost_(.*)-gcc.*/\1/'`
>         ln -s ${lib} /usr/lib/libboost_${name}-mt.so
>     done
>     ldconfig
>
> Execute as root and try building again.
>
> Cheers,
> Paul.
