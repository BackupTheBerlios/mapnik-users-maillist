Justin,

Paul's suggestion is spot on.  I'd add that www.spatialreference.org  
is a handy web api for that same trick.

You can plug your some of your .prj text in the search like:

http://www.spatialreference.org/ref/epsg/?search=NAD_1983_StatePlane_Maryland_FIPS_1900_Feet&srtext=Search+EPSG+References

and get back:

http://www.spatialreference.org/ref/epsg/102685/
or
http://www.spatialreference.org/ref/epsg/102685/proj4/


-dane
