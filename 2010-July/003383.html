Iván Sánchez Ortega (OpenStreetMap España) and I are teaching a workshop on OpenStreetMap rendering at FOSS4G 2010 in Barcelona.

The workshop is on Sept 6th, 2010. It is filling up quickly but still has a few spaces remaining.

If you are considering attending FOSS4G, we'd love to see you at the workshop.

More info at: 

http://2010.foss4g.org/workshop.php
http://2010.foss4g.org/workshop03.php

If you can't make it, or don't plan to attend FOSS4G, no worries. We anticipate that all materials will be available online for download following the conference. They will consist of step-by-step documentation and tutorials (HTML & PDF), an Ubuntu Lucid VirtualBox machine with all packages installed, and links to mac osx/windows installers.
