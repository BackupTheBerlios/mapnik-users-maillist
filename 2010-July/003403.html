The order of <Layer> elements and <StyleName> therein decides rendering 
order, not the order of <Style> elements.

However, by looking at your stylesheet, your smaller texts are probably 
dropped because they don't pass the [Type]=5 filter, and that allows the 
subsequent larger texts to render.
