On 11/18/10 09:48, Jin Kang wrote:
> From your experience, in general how long does it take to render one tile? Thanks in advance!

A normal OSM tile server setup (with mod_tile and renderd or tirex) 
would always render "metatiles", comprising 8x8 normal tiles. The 
rendering speed depends on the style and zoom level, and of course on 
your hardware. The bottleneck is usually the database access. If you 
have a fast hard disk or a lot of cache memory then you can expect to be 
able to render 1-2 metatiles in the OSM standard style per second on 
average. If you have an average hard disk and not so much memory, you 
should perhaps assume that one metatile takes 5-10 seconds to render but 
since one metatile has 64 tiles, that should still give you around 10 
tiles per second even on a small machine.

Rendering tiles individually (i.e. not using metatiles) will take much 
longer.
