>From my experience with the German data of OpenStreetMap, it takes about 60
- 120 Minutes to render the zoom level 1 - 15.  In this case my database
contained only the German subset and I was rendering 1 - 4 63 * 63 meta
tiles at once, writing the resulting PNG data in large chunks into a single
file.
Rendering and writing single tiles takes about 2 days on the other hand.
Importing the complete planet.osm instead of your country extract also slows
down the rendering process considerably.
