Hi,

I'm an absolute newby with mapnik, but this is just about computing 
exponential costs.

Suppose you have japan in 1 tile, and you like to render 10 levels of 
zoom. And hen you zoom each tile it generates 4 tiles, so:

1 + 4 + 16 + 64 + 256 + 1024 + 4096 ....
4¹+ 4²+4³+4⁴ ....

so for level 10 you have to render 4¹⁰ tiles = 1048576

if one tile takes just one second to render you will need 1048576 
seconds to render just level 10, this are 291 hours or 12 days.

This is just an example, i do not know exactly if when zooming one tile 
explode to four, but the computing cost is 100% exponential.
