For the record, I've got the solution with the help from Artem(many thanks):
Just  apply to all boost libs:

ln -s  /usr/lib/libboost_<lib>-gcc-mt-1_33_1.so /usr/lib/libboost_<lib>-mt.so
and then:
ldd /usr/lib/libboost_<lib>.mt.so
