The Mapnik portfile has gone unmaintained as I've focused energy on  
the binary packages available at http://dbsgeo.com/downloads.

Even if the portfile were maintained i dont recommend macports as  
their installation approach for boost is error prone.  But in this  
case, essentially the problem with the port is that boost was upgraded  
by macports and the pymapnik portfile still uses Mapnik 0.6.x. So you  
should file a ticket at macports.org to upgrade the mapnik portfile.
