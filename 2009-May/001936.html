The trick is you need to configure your Client (OpenLayers) to make  
requests in the bounding coordinates of the SRS.

To get mercator(3395) coordinates for this example try:

 >>> import mapnik
 >>> bbox = mapnik.Envelope(37.265625,55.72265625,37.44140625,55.898437)
 >>> bbox.forward(mapnik.Projection('+init=epsg:3395'))
Envelope(4148390.39909,7468065.42159,4167958.27833,7502814.00493)
 >>>

What happens if you use those for your BBOX value?

Also, make sure 3395 is listed in your 'allowedepsgcodes' parameter in  
your ogcserver.conf

Lastly, I notice the 'reproject=True' flag in that url. I'm actually  
not familiar with how that works but I think it is just an OL option  
and has no effect on the WMS server (http://trac.openlayers.org/ticket/881 
).
