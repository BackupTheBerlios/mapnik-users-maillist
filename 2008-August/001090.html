Looking for a Mapnik Consultant

We're looking for a Mapnik consultant for a small project that has the
potential to turn into a larger project. We've recently done a lot of work
plotting data on maps for international development organizations to help
them better visualize their data. For one of our recent projects, we ran the
mapping stack on Mapnik and had it plug into a Drupal website. The results
were stunning, and we'd like to delve into Mapnik to see what else we can do
with it.

We're a online strategy shop located in Washington, DC that builds creative
online communications solutions for progressive organizations, mostly using
Drupal. Our focus on the international development community has led us to
be very interested in mapping and to become the lead geospatial developers
in the Drupal community. You can read more about us at
www.developmentseed.org and be sure to check out our blog
http://www.developmentseed.org/tags/mapping
